# Cobalt Executive Deck（钴蓝商策）

Use this style for business-facing reports, product portfolios, executive summaries, company profiles, partnership proposals, and work updates that need a polished corporate tone.

## Best For

- workplace reports, monthly or quarterly reviews
- business proposals, partnership decks, client-facing plans
- product introductions, product catalogs, launch pages
- company profiles, service packages, capability summaries
- strategy roadmaps, KPI reporting, delivery plans

## Visual Language

- cool grey stage background with white or very light grey business canvases
- cobalt blue as the main accent, used in panels, bars, labels, and vertical brand blocks
- black, graphite, and monochrome media areas for contrast
- large bold English headings with compact Chinese support text
- vertical brand typography, contents cards, product panels, KPI modules, and roadmap grids
- product-like abstract CSS objects instead of borrowed product imagery

## Layout Grammar

- Use a clean corporate slide rhythm: cover, contents, background, product/module, metrics, roadmap, scenarios, closing.
- Keep cobalt blue strong but controlled; do not turn every panel blue.
- Use black/white media placeholders when real product images are unavailable.
- Pair large display headlines with short executive notes.
- Use KPI cards for quantified outcomes and roadmap blocks for phased plans.
- For product sections, combine one large visual area with stacked feature/module rows.

## Interaction Grammar

Interactions should feel executive and useful:

- scroll reveal for each board
- animated hero product/object motion
- clickable contents cards that jump to sections
- product/module tabs that update a detail panel
- scenario cards that update a summary note
- floating top/next navigation controls
- reduced-motion support

Avoid playful stickers, casual doodles, overly academic tables, or warm editorial palettes. The tone should be businesslike, clean, and confident.

## Content Mapping

When adapting real user content:

- company/product name -> cover title and vertical brand block
- business context -> background/about section
- product or service modules -> product tabs and matrix cards
- achievements, budget, growth, efficiency -> KPI cards
- project schedule or implementation plan -> roadmap
- audience or client scenarios -> scenario cards
- call to action -> closing board

## Common Page Types

- Business cover
- Contents / executive path
- Background and objective
- Product/service center
- KPI dashboard
- Roadmap / delivery plan
- Scenario cards
- Closing / thank-you page
