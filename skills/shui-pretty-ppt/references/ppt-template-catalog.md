# Shui Pretty PPT Template Catalog

Use this catalog before selecting a template. The library is intentionally split into two presentation modules.

## Module A · Creator, Personal Brand, Portfolio

These templates are more colorful, memorable, editorial, and suitable for public sharing.

| # | Template | Chinese Name | Best For | Color / Visual Keywords |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | Pastel Blockfolio | 粉彩拼贴志 | self-media tutorials, case studies, workflow recaps, portfolio-like explanation decks | off-white paper, thick black borders, pastel pink, mint, yellow, sticky notes |
| 02 | Blush Editorial | 暖粉编辑志 | brand content, recommendation lists, catalogs, polished content pages | cream-pink canvas, white editorial rows, black display type, bright-pink accents |
| 03 | Mono Curve Slides | 墨线白稿 | clean product updates, course explainers, slide-gallery stories | cool grey stage, white 16:9 canvases, hand-drawn curves, sparse color washes |
| 08 | Ribbon Tab Brochure | 彩签页报 | project brochures, product/service explainers, operations reports, external proposals | colored ribbon tabs, pastel backgrounds, fine black frames, data panels |
| 11 | Blue Growth Deck | 蓝色增长稿 | AI products, growth reports, GEO recaps, friendly product launches | sky blue, deep navy, lime green, blush pink, bubbles, cards, phone-like UI |
| 12 | Garden Pop Landing | 花园跳色长页 | creator courses, self-media tutorials, education products, campaign decks | orange-yellow stage, grass green, pink flowers, mint panels, playful garden map |

### Module A Selection Notes

- Choose **Pastel Blockfolio** when the content is process-heavy and needs a handmade tutorial feel.
- Choose **Blush Editorial** when the content should feel premium, curated, or brand/editorial.
- Choose **Mono Curve Slides** when the user wants something closest to a clean slide gallery.
- Choose **Ribbon Tab Brochure** when the deck is closer to a brochure or service package.
- Choose **Blue Growth Deck** when the topic is AI, growth, product, or creator business.
- Choose **Garden Pop Landing** when the deck needs the strongest color memory and launch energy.

### Module B Selection Notes

- Choose **One Dot Cinnabar** for restrained, formal, red-accent work reports.
- Choose **Ivory Research Deck** when evidence, methods, tables, or research logic matter.
- Choose **Cobalt Executive Deck** for business-facing product/company/proposal decks.
- Choose **Coral Startup Deck** for warmer team or startup-style reporting.
- Choose **Sapphire Defense Deck** for academic or thesis-defense structure.
- Choose **Vermilion Civic Deck** for administrative, civic, or formal red-theme reports.

## Output Standard

A finished Shui Pretty PPT deck should have:

- a clear cover page
- a visible section or agenda structure
- no raw long-document dumping
- a consistent template-specific color system
- working browser navigation or scroll/slide controls
- local assets stored in the output directory
- no unresolved placeholders
